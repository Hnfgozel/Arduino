

Pin	Label	Arduino Connection
1	AO	    Analog input
2	G		Ground (GND)
3	+		5 volt power
4	DO		Digital input


Testing

Place a magnetic source near the switch to test.

there are two basic sets of values, the first is the digital value the second is the analog value

1/ The reed switch open/ no magnet - 0 and 1023
2/ The reed switch closed/ magnet present - 1 and 0
